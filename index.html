Biography

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, supervised by Professor Jing Jiang. Before that, I obtained my B.E. degree of Software Engineering at Central South University in 2019.

I am also fortunate to work closely with Tianyu Pang, Chao Du, Qian Liu and Min Lin.

I research Interpretability (Data-Centric) and Safety.
